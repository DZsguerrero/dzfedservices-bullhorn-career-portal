About Day & Zimmermann Accessibility Notice

Day & Zimmermann's Engineering, Construction and Maintenance (ECM) group provides a broad spectrum of integrated, end-to-end solutions to the power, process and industrial markets. Engineering and design services include all disciplines as well as comprehensive EPC (engineering, procurement and construction) solutions. Flexible construction solutions range from general contracting to construction management, as a well as diverse fabrication and machining capabilities. Maintenance services include modifications, major construction, as well as valve, condenser, and radiological services. Day & Zimmermann is ranked by Engineering News-Record as the top maintenance contractor in the power market and a top 10 design firm in chemical plants and industrial process.

Day & Zimmermann is committed to maintaining an inclusive workforce, where employees are hired, retained, compensated and promoted based on their contributions to our Company. Our collective strength is rooted in over 110 years of diverse employees and businesses, commitment to success, and delivery on promises made.

Federal and State Equal Employment Opportunity laws prohibit employment discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, age, national origin, citizenship status, veteran status and disability status. Day & Zimmermann is committed to providing an equal opportunity work environment in full compliance with these laws.

If you are an individual with a disability and you require an accommodation in the application process, please send us an email at accommodations@dayzim.com.
